About Our Book Club

Learn more about who we are and what inspires this community.

Our Mission

The BookClub application was created to bring readers together in a simple, engaging, and organized way. Whether you're discovering new authors, sharing your favorite novels, or participating in monthly reading challenges, our goal is to make your book club experience seamless and enjoyable.

What We Offer

  • A curated list of top books each month
  • Easy navigation between pages using a clean, modern layout
  • Simple access to contact information and club details
  • A growing platform designed with Spring Boot and Thymeleaf

Our Story

This project began as part of a server-side development course, but quickly grew into something more meaningful — a space where readers can connect, explore, and share their love of books. As the application evolves, we hope to continue adding features that support book clubs of all sizes.
